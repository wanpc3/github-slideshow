About

Instead of playing Dota 2, Aiman (Berlin) also became a coach of Combo Wombo Dota 2 team. Then, Berlin volunteered himself to become a coach of Combo Wombo Dota 2 team until now. He is very inspiring person among his teammates. He has many knowledge about Dota 2 and He always guide his player from a-z. In order to increase his income, Berlin worked as a rider of Foodpanda for 3 hours at Shah Alam area.
