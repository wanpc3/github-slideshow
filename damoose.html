Story

Nabil better known by his alias DaMoose is Malaysian professional CreativeDestruction player. Instead of playing CS:GO, Nabil also decided to play CreativeDestruction and he use DaMoose as his profile name on the game.

DaMoose was selected by Combo Wombo team to play both CS:GO and CreativeDestructions too. Since then, he felt the pressure for being on both teams but he managed to play it better because of his skills that he brought from CS:GO
