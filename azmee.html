Story

Hasmee Azmee (azmee) is Malaysian Dota 2 Player of Combo Wombo team. He started playing Dota 2 since he was at High School (SMK Seksyen 7). He practiced Dota 2 with his friends and learned from other professional Dota 2 players like Ceb, Nisha and Resolut1on.

azmee's career on Dota 2 began after he met Firdaus (lilymac) and Ilhan (Freezy) at school and they decided to play Dota 2 together after school. azmee currently play role as Offlane of Combo Wombo Dota 2 team.
