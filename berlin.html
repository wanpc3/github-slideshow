Story

Aiman, better known by his alias Berlin is professional Dota 2 player of Combo Wombo Dota 2 team. He played Dota 2 since 3rd semester of his diploma at UITM SHAH ALAM. He's played and trained Dota 2 with friends at the campus.

Berlin's journey on Dota 2 began after he met Ilhan (Freezy) after joining this Dota 2 team. This was to be the first long relationship between Berlin and Freezy. Berlin currently become Captain and play role as a Carry of Combo Wombo Dota 2 team.
