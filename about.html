Combo Wombo

is Professional Esports team, founded in Malaysia. This team took part in tournaments like DOTA 2, CS GO, PUBG and CreativeDestruction. Combo Wombo currently become a world's best Esports team.

Origin

Combo Wombo Esports team was founded in Shah Alam, Selangor by Wan Ilhan Idriss, Firdaus Ismanizal and Akmal Hakim Azuan. This team is established in 2020. We currently looking for partners to make our team become successful one day.

Combo Wombo currently inactive during this pandemic crisis but we currently join a LAN tournaments to battle against more Esports Professional teams like Team Liquid, Cloud9 and Team Secret.
