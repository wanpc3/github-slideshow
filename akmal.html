Story

Akmal Hakim Azuan better known by his alias Danny Ruccaria IV, is professional CS:GO player of Combo Wombo CS:GO team. He played CS:GO since he was high school at SMK Seksyen 7. He always spend his time at his laptop to play CS:GO after finish his homework.

Danny Ruccaria IV journey on Esports began when he was selected by his friend, Nabil (ZEMoose) to join Combo Wombo CS:GO team. This was to be the beginning of their long relationship on Esports. Danny Ruccaria IV currently play role as Rifler (Entry Fragger) of Combo Wombo CS:GO team.
